- Serif or friendly fonts
- Organic shapes, food imagery

### Medical:
- Calming blues/teals
- Clean professional layouts
- Trust-building elements

### Professional:
- Navy/gold/gray palettes
- Serif or professional sans-serif

### Beauty:

- Elegant typography
- Glamorous elements

### Home:
- Warm neutrals, wood tones

- Lifestyle imagery

### Events:
- Vibrant celebratory colors
- Dynamic layouts
- Joyful energy

### Education:
- Friendly approachable colors
- Clear readable layouts
- Academic feel
